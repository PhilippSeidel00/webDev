Arminius

January 2022

Developed as part of the historically accurate game jam 5. As a team of four friends we decided to participate. All of us were new to game development and had no prior experience. In the game you aim to hide your troops in order to surprise the roman legions marching through the Teutoburg Forest and thus win the day for the Germanes. While nowhere near polished it nevertheless was a great opportunity to learn about game development and we had a great time doing so.

Give the game a try here.

The source code is publicly available go have a look if you dare!
